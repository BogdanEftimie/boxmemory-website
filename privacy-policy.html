Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

BoxMemory ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

1. Overview

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. BoxMemory is an offline-first application. All data you create within the app — including box names, location tags, notes, and photos — is stored exclusively on your personal device. No data is ever sent to our servers or any third-party service.

2. Permissions & Device Access

The app requests the following permissions, all of which are used solely for core functionality:

Permission Purpose
Camera Photograph the exterior of your boxes and their contents
Photo Library Attach existing photos from your device to boxes
Photo Library Add Save photos taken within the app

You may revoke any of these permissions in your device settings at any time.

3. Data Storage

  • Box data (names, locations, notes, timestamps) is stored locally using AsyncStorage on your device.
  • Photos are stored in the app's private document directory on your device.
  • Custom location tags are stored locally on your device.
  • All data remains on your device and is never uploaded anywhere.

4. Data Backup & Export

The app includes a built-in backup feature that:

  • Compresses your box data and photos into a ZIP file.
  • Allows you to optionally share that file using your device's native share sheet (Files, iCloud, email, or any app that accepts files).

This is a user-initiated action — we do not automatically back up your data. Any data shared to cloud services is done so directly by you through your device's system share sheet, and is subject to those services' respective privacy policies.

The app also includes an import & restore feature that reads a previously exported ZIP file from your device and reconstructs your box data and photos locally. This process occurs entirely on your device — no data is transmitted to external servers.

5. Freemium Model

BoxMemory offers a free tier with up to 3 boxes. A one-time purchase (Pro upgrade) unlocks unlimited boxes, backup & export, and removes ads. No subscriptions or recurring charges.

If the Pro upgrade involves in-app purchases, those transactions are processed by the respective app store (Apple App Store or Google Play Store) and are subject to their terms and privacy policies.

6. Third-Party Services

BoxMemory does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

The backup feature uses your device's native share sheet to let you save or send your backup file to cloud storage services (such as Files, iCloud, email, Google Drive, or Dropbox) of your choice. These integrations are controlled entirely by you, and we do not access, store, or transmit your cloud credentials.

7. Data Deletion

  • Individual boxes: Delete specific boxes from within the app.
  • All data: Uninstalling the app removes all stored data from your device.
  • Photos: Photos taken within the app are stored in the app's private directory and are removed upon uninstallation.

8. Children's Privacy

BoxMemory is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on our website and updating the "Last updated" date.
